# MiniMax Design System

## Visual Identity

MiniMax employs a white-dominant layout with colorful product card accents, combining Apple-inspired clarity with approachable, rounded design. The aesthetic prioritizes negative space, letting vibrant product cards function as visual anchors against pure white (`#ffffff`) backgrounds.

## Typography

The system uses four distinct typefaces strategically:

- **DM Sans**: Primary UI workhorse (body, navigation, buttons) - weights: 400, 500, 600, 700
- **Outfit**: Display headings with geometric elegance - weights: 400, 500, 600, 700
- **Poppins**: Mid-tier headings and feature labels - weights: 400, 500, 600
- **Roboto**: Data-heavy contexts - weights: 400, 500, 600

### Font Sizes

- Display: 80px (line-height: 1.10)
- H1: 32px
- H2: 28px–31px
- H3: 24px
- Body large: 18px–20px
- Body: 16px (line-height: 1.50)
- Small: 14px
- Caption: 12px–13px (line-height: 1.70)
- Micro: 10px–11px

## Component Characteristics

- **Buttons**: Pill geometry (9999px radius) for navigation; primary uses brand blue
- **Product Cards**: 20–24px radius with purple-tinted shadows; vibrant gradient backgrounds
- **Navigation**: Clean, minimal with DM Sans; pill-shaped CTAs

## Key Principles

- Avoid colored backgrounds in main sections
- Avoid sharp corners on product cards
- Avoid weight 700 for headings (use 500 as default emphasis)
- Let product illustrations provide visual interest against the white canvas
- Maintain light, airy feel through low-opacity shadows and generous whitespace
