# Together AI Design System Summary

## Core Visual Identity

Together AI presents itself as an "AI-Native Cloud" through a striking dual-atmosphere design. The brand combines "soft pink-blue-lavender gradients" with sharp typography to make enterprise infrastructure feel approachable yet authoritative.

## Color Foundation

**Primary Colors:**
- Dark Blue (#010120) — the distinctive midnight-blue used throughout dark sections
- Pure White (#ffffff) — light canvas backgrounds
- Brand Magenta (#ef2cc1) and Orange (#fc4c02) — reserved exclusively for gradient illustrations

**Supporting Palette:**
- Soft Lavender (#bdbbff) for subtle accents
- Dark-blue-tinted shadows (rgba(1, 1, 32, 0.1)) for elevation

## Typography System

Two typeface families establish hierarchy:
- **"The Future"** — all display and body text with consistently negative letter-spacing
- **PP Neue Montreal Mono** — uppercase technical labels with positive letter-spacing

Key principle: "Aggressive negative tracking (-1.92px)" on headlines creates dense, modern text blocks.

## Component Standards

Buttons use sharp 4px radius with frosted glass effects on dark surfaces. Cards employ the distinctive dark-blue shadow rather than generic black. Stats display large numbers prominently as visual anchors.

## Layout Philosophy

Spacing follows an 8px base unit with "generous" section separation (80–120px). The design maintains distinct light zones (business messaging) and dark zones (research content), creating what the system describes as "optimistic breathing room."

## Critical Restraints

The system explicitly avoids pill-shaped corners, bold typefaces, warm shadows, and magenta/orange in UI elements—these brand colors appear in illustrations only.
